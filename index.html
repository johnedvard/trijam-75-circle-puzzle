How to play

The goal is to get all the three squares into the black and white squares

Player 1 controls the white square

  • using "a", "s", "d", "w" to move
  • using "q" to shring the circle, and "e" to enlarge the circle

Player 2 controls the two blue squares

  • using "o" and "p" to move the dark blue square along the circle
  • using "i" and "j" to move the light blue square along the circle

This game prototype was made in 3 hours and 30 minutes.
